Academic Programs:

Offers B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, and Ph.D. programs. Covers engineering disciplines like Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, and more. Emphasis on both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

Research and Development:

Strong focus on research with various funded projects. Collaboration with industries and research organizations. Facilities for cutting-edge research in emerging technologies.

Campus Facilities:

Modern classrooms and laboratories. Advanced computing centers and a well-stocked library. Sports complexes, hostels, and recreational areas.

Industry Collaboration:

Partnerships with leading companies for internships and projects. Regular guest lectures and workshops by industry experts. Industry-driven curriculum updates and real-world problem-solving.

Student Life:

Active student clubs and societies. Cultural events, technical fests, and sports activities. Opportunities for leadership and community service.

Accreditations and Rankings:

Accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA). Consistently ranked among the top engineering institutions in Karnataka. Recognized for excellence in teaching, research, and infrastructure.

Alumni Network:

Strong network of alumni in various sectors. Alumni often engage in mentorship and guest lectures. Support for career placements and professional growth.
